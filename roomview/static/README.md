## ğŸ® How to Use

### For Customers
1. **Start Shopping**: Open the application to see the room selection
2. **Choose a Room**: Click on any of the 4 room cards (Kitchen, Living Room, Bedroom, Bathroom)
3. **Explore Areas**: Click on numbered hotspots (1-4) to discover products
4. **Browse Products**: View product details, prices, and descriptions
5. **Add to Cart**: Use the shopping cart to manage your selections
6. **Checkout**: Complete your purchase through the cart sidebar

### For Administrators
1. **Access Admin Panel**: Click the gear icon in the navigation

3. **View Analytics**: Monitor sales data and key metrics
4. **Track Inventory**: Keep an eye on stock levels and alerts
